How Colors Works

Move a primary color tile onto a legal neighboring color to score and transform the board.

  • Primary colors are red, blue, and yellow.
  • Mixing two different neighboring primary tiles creates a mixed color.
  • White tiles add to the score and can never be landed on.
  • Path and target-color rules determine whether a move is legal.
